SA Humane Society RWD

I acted as project manager working with two other UX designers and researchers for this project. I was involved in every process from user research, defining personas, flows and wireframing, to prototyping and UI design. The following sections depict the process towards our final product.

What's The Problem?

The Problem

The San Antonio Humane Society website lacks current pet information, and users have found it difficult to accurately search for pet preferences which has contributed to a decrease in adoption numbers.

The Solution

We put interactive adoption information tabs directly on the hero page, provided search filters, and updated pictures and pet information which will help users learn more about their prospective pet.

Research

User Interviews

By conducting user interviews and surveys, we were able to get a better grasp of how users could interact with the website in more useful ways. We learned that users would greatly value pet search and filters, more updated info on pets, and less paperwork.

Competitor Analysis

After conducting a competitor analysis, we observed that some websites such as “Paws Ranch Rescue”:, and “Animal Cares Service” do well at providing Pet Finder Tools, and other tools that help the user through the adoption process.

Story Board

After conducting extensive researcxh, we were able to synthesize our data and develop a storyboard. Our storyboard depicts our User’s adoption journey using our improved redesign.
